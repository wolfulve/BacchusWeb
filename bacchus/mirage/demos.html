DEMO 1

This demo, originally developed for Viacom New Media, was the first demo commissioned by Rendition, Inc., to showcase their Vérité V2000 3D accelerator chip.

The demo shows a cathedral environment with several 3D gates, constant and partial transparency, true reflections, bilinear and trilinear filtering, specular highlighting and dynamic lighting

This demo also highlights some of the technology that will be used in the Mirage concept, e.g., 3D gates.


System Requirements
  • Pentium 166Mhz or greater
  • 3D graphics card with a Rendition Vérité V2100 or V2200
  • Windows 95 or MS-DOS 5.0 or greater
  • 32 MB RAM minimum
  • 2 or 3 button mouse
  • mirage-v2-demo.zip

DEMO 2

This is an older, unaccelerated demo that was originally designed as a prototype for Viacom New Media.

The demo features two completely different environments and several compelling examples of 3D gates, including a rotating recursive gate.


System Requirements
  • Pentium 166Mhz or greater
  • Graphics card with VESA modes
  • Windows 95 or MS-DOS 5.0 or greater
  • 32 MB RAM minimum
  • 2 or 3 button mouse
